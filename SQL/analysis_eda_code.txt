
- Inizio la mia analisi controllando il tasso di conservazione degli utenti, formulando le seguenti domande


--- Q1: How many users left the program? - Quanti utenti hanno abbandonato il programma?

SELECT 
	clean_user_churn,
	COUNT(*) AS users
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_churn
ORDER BY users DESC;



--- Q2: How many users are in each engagement category and which activity groups have the highest churn rate? - Quanti utenti ci sono in ciascuna categoria di engagement e quali gruppi di attività hanno il tasso di abbandono più alto?

SELECT 
	clean_user_activity,
	clean_user_churn,
	COUNT(*) AS users
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_activity <> 'Unknown' AND clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_activity, clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_user_activity, clean_user_churn DESC;



--- Q3: Which age segments are leaving the program the most? - Quali fasce d'età abbandonano maggiormente il programma?

SELECT
    c.age_range,
    u.clean_user_churn,
    COUNT(*) AS users
FROM clean_users AS u
JOIN clean_customers AS c
    ON u.user_id = c.user_id
WHERE clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY c.age_range, u.clean_user_churn
ORDER BY c.age_range, u.clean_user_churn DESC;



--- Q4: Which diabetes population is hardest to retain? - Quale gruppo di persone con diabete è il più difficile da trattenere nello studio?

SELECT
	clean_diagnosis,
	clean_user_churn,
	COUNT(*) AS users
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_diagnosis <> 'Unknown' AND clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_diagnosis, clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_diagnosis, clean_user_churn DESC;




- in seguito ho deciso di capire le motivazioni per cui gli utenti abbandonano il programma, attraverso le seguenti domande


--- Q5: Are less engaged users more likely to leave the program? - Gli utenti meno coinvolti hanno una maggiore probabilità di abbandonare il programma?

SELECT 
clean_user_activity,
COUNT(*) AS total_users,
SUM(CASE
        WHEN clean_user_churn = 'Yes' THEN 1
        ELSE 0
        END
    ) AS churned_users,
ROUND(SUM(CASE
              WHEN clean_user_churn = 'Yes' THEN 1
              ELSE 0
              END
          ) * 100.0 / COUNT(*), 2
     ) AS churn_rate_percentage
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown' AND clean_user_activity <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_activity
ORDER BY churn_rate_percentage DESC;



--- Q6: Are retained users showing stronger long-term diabetes control? - Gli utenti mantenuti nel programma mostrano un migliore controllo del diabete a lungo termine?

SELECT
    clean_user_churn,
    ROUND(CAST(AVG(initial_hba1c) AS NUMERIC), 2) AS avg_initial_hba1c,
    ROUND(CAST(AVG(current_hba1c) AS NUMERIC), 2) AS avg_current_hba1c,
    ROUND(CAST(AVG(initial_hba1c - current_hba1c) AS NUMERIC), 2) AS avg_hba1c_reduction
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_user_churn DESC;
--- La funzione AVG restituiva un valore in double precision, quindi l'ho convertito a numeric prima dell’arrotondamento a due decimali.



--- Q7: Are users who improve their glucose control more likely to remain enrolled? - Gli utenti che migliorano il controllo della glicemia hanno una maggiore probabilità di rimanere iscritti al programma?

SELECT
	clean_user_churn,
	ROUND(CAST(AVG(initial_glycemia_rate) AS NUMERIC), 1) AS avg_initial_glycemia,
	ROUND(CAST(AVG(final_glycemia_rate) AS NUMERIC), 1) AS avg_final_glycemia
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_user_churn DESC;





- Ho anche deciso di analizzare l’efficacia del trattamento sugli utenti attraverso queste domande


--- Q8: Which patient groups benefit most from the digital therapeutic program and experience the greatest improvement in long-term diabetes control? - Quali gruppi di pazienti traggono i maggiori benefici dal programma terapeutico digitale e mostrano il miglioramento più significativo nel controllo del diabete a lungo termine?

SELECT
	clean_diagnosis,
	ROUND(CAST(AVG(initial_hba1c - current_hba1c) AS numeric), 2) AS avg_hba1c_reduction
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_diagnosis <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_diagnosis
ORDER BY avg_hba1c_reduction DESC;



--- Q9: Does higher engagement translate into better clinical outcomes? - Un maggiore coinvolgimento si traduce in migliori esiti clinici?

SELECT
	clean_user_activity,
	ROUND(CAST(AVG(initial_hba1c) AS numeric), 2) AS avg_initial_hba1c,
	ROUND(CAST(AVG(current_hba1c) AS numeric), 2) AS avg_current_hba1c
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_activity <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_activity
ORDER BY avg_initial_hba1c DESC;





- Infine, ho deciso di ricavare alcuni insight utili per il business


--- Q10: Which enrollment channels bring the most users into the program? - Quali canali di iscrizione portano il maggior numero di utenti al programma?

SELECT
	clean_enrollment_channel,
	COUNT(*) AS users
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_enrollment_channel <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_enrollment_channel
ORDER BY users DESC;



--- Q11: Are some acquisition channels attracting users with higher churn risk? - Ci sono dei canali di acquisizione che attraggono utenti con un rischio di abbandono più elevato?

SELECT
	clean_enrollment_channel,
	clean_user_churn,
	COUNT(*) AS users,
	ROUND(COUNT(*) * 100.0 / SUM(COUNT(*)) OVER(PARTITION BY clean_enrollment_channel), 2) AS percentage
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_enrollment_channel <> 'Unknown' AND clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_enrollment_channel, clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_enrollment_channel, clean_user_churn DESC;



--- Q12: Which treatment plans achieve better retention? - Quali piani di trattamento ottengono una migliore conservazione di utenti?

SELECT
    clean_treatment_plan,
    clean_user_churn,
    COUNT(*) AS users
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_treatment_plan <> 'Unknown' AND clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_treatment_plan, clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_treatment_plan, clean_user_churn DESC;



--- Q13: How long do users stay in the program on average before leaving? - Per quanto tempo, in media, gli utenti rimangono nel programma prima di abbandonarlo?

SELECT
	clean_user_churn,
	ROUND(CAST(AVG(months_in_program) AS NUMERIC), 1) AS avg_months
FROM clean_users
WHERE clean_user_churn <> 'Unknown'
GROUP BY clean_user_churn
ORDER BY clean_user_churn DESC;



- Ho deciso di rimuovere tutti i valori null o sconosciuti da ogni query perché, in caso contrario, rischierei di includere categorie non utili o che non rappresentano lo stato reale, soprattutto in alcune query specifiche. Detto questo, i valori nulli possono comunque rappresentare un’informazione importante, infatti li includerò nella visualizzazione dei dati.
